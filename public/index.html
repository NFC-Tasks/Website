NFCompanion

automating tasks with a single click

We're using NFC (near field communication) tags and an Android phone to facilitate easy task automation. By putting down a phone on the NFC tag (attached to the car/object in question), the app detects the tag and performs a series of actions that it's programmed to do. For example, on the car, it detects the temperature outside, and adjusts a Nest thermostat inside the house to an appropriate temperature. In addition to that, it starts Google Maps and uses your location to navigate you home.

Here's a video of our app in action. It first has you select the "car," which is the object to which our NFC tag is attached to. It then prompts you to put your phone on the tag attached to your car, as it'll trigger the scan and start the actions.
"Preparing the tasks" triggers a detection of the outside temperature, and adjusts the Nest thermostat inside your house accordingly. Then, the app starts a Google maps navigation and helps you get directions to get home.
